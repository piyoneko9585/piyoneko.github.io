Web Banner Design for Digital Business Strategy 1-Day Seminar (Proposal)

Banner Design Sample(Proposal)

  • Project Type: Web banner design for a fictional “Digital Business Strategy 1-Day Seminar”(designed based on a provided brief)
  • Objective: Focused on clear value communication and improving CTR while maintaining consistency with the landing page

Design Approach

Messaging:

  • Top-left(company logo): majimon
  • Upper center: Double Your Sales & Save Time for Smarter Business Management
  • Center (main title):Digital Business Strategy 1-Day Seminar
  • Below title:A Web Strategy Course for Busy Business Owners
  • Lower section:Yasuhiro Yamagami(name)
  • Below the name(small text): Digital Marketer/CEO of majimon
  • Bottom:Tokyo | May 20 & 22
  • Bottom-right (CTA):Register Now

Background:

  • A modern and professional style was used to target a business audience
  • Polygon pattern applied to create a dynamic and sophisticated atmosphere
  • Charcoal gray background chosen to convey trust and stability
  • CTA elements highlighted with a contrasting red color for visibility

Layout:

  • Added a benefit-focused headline above the seminar title to improve clarity
  • Increased visual hierarchy using font size to emphasize key message (“2x Your Sales & Save Time”)
  • Placed a directional arrow under the CTA to guide user attention toward the landing page

Typography:

  • Bold sans-serif font selected for readability and modern taste
  • Font weight used to clarify visual hierarchy
  • Consistent typography to maintain a clean and structured layout

Result

  • Clear communication of key benefits within a limited space
  • Improved readability and visual hierarchy
  • Designed with CTR optimization and user flow in mind
